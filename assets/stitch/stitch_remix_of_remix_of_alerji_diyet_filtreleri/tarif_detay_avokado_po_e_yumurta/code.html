Instructions

1

Toast the bread

Place the sourdough slices in a toaster or grill pan until they are golden brown and crispy.

2

Mash the avocado

In a small bowl, combine avocado, lemon juice, salt, and red pepper flakes. Mash until creamy but slightly chunky.

3

Poach the eggs

Gently drop eggs into simmering water with a splash of vinegar. Cook for 3-4 minutes until the whites are set but yolks remain soft.

4

Assemble & Garnish

Spread avocado on toast, top with a poached egg, and finish with a drizzle of olive oil and extra flakes.
